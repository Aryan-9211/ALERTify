About

ALERTify uses Geofencing and Beacon Technology to achieve its Tasks.

Geofencing is a location-based service in which an app or other software uses GPS, RFID, Wi-Fi or cellular data to trigger a action when a mobile device or RFID tag enters or exits a virtual boundary set up around a geographical location, known as a geofence.
